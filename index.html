What´s the purpose of CrowdTrip?

Instant joining

You need to get from A to B right now? No problem! With CrowdTrip you don´t need to plan ahead or make any reservations. Just join the next ride!

Easy to use

No confusing extras. We focused on what matters: Matching passengers with drivers.

One step further

We combine the routes of different drivers to offer a unique experience. Even if no one drives your exact route, you can still get to your destination by an automatic combination of multiple drivers.

Impressions

Take a look at the app.

  • Offer a trip

    You are a driver? Just offer a trip in five seconds. Set your destinatin, the maximum diversion you are willing to drive and the price for your passengers.

  • Join a trip

    You are a passenger? Search and join for existing trips. Just enter your destination and we will match you to a driver or try to combine the routes of different drivers.

  • Manage your passengers

    As a driver, you can see how much you will earn from this trip and where to pick up and drop off your passengers.

  • Details to your trip

    As a passenger, you can see you drivers and the routes you will drive with them.
